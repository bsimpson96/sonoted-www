# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-05*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** SoNoted uses AI to surface and activate everything you've ever written.
**What it does:** SoNoted is an AI-powered knowledge activation layer that connects your notes across tools and time. It uses semantic search to find what you meant (not just what you typed), automatically links related ideas, and synthesizes answers from your own writing — not the internet's.
**Product category:** Knowledge activation (new category — not PKM, not AI chat, not search)
**Product type:** SaaS web app (early access / pre-launch)
**Business model:** Pricing TBD. Likely two tiers:
- Individual/Prosumer: ~$15-20/mo
- SEC/Compliance Professional: ~$75-100/mo (RIAs, broker-dealers — regulatory record-keeping obligations make this a distinct high-value segment)

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Two segments:
1. B2C prosumer -- knowledge workers, consultants, researchers, founders who are drowning in notes
2. SEC-regulated entities -- PE firms, RIAs, broker-dealers, where note-taking is both constant and compliance-sensitive (Rule 17a-4, FINRA 4511). Founder has direct traction here via his role as a tech advisor at a PE firm. Warm relationships, not cold market.
**Decision-makers:** Individual professionals (prosumer); for financial services: partners, principals, compliance officers who are themselves power note-takers
**Primary use case:** Finding and connecting notes across tools, time, and mental contexts — especially notes you've forgotten you wrote
**Jobs to be done:**
- "Help me find that thing I know I wrote down"
- "Help me synthesize everything I've collected on a topic into something useful"
- "Help me use my notes to do better work — proposals, research, thinking"
**Use cases:**
- Writing a client proposal with all relevant past notes surfaced automatically
- Synthesizing years of research from across multiple apps into an answer
- Recovering a half-remembered idea, phrase, or concept by typing a fragment
- PE/finance specific: surfacing everything written about a portfolio company before a board call; connecting deal memos, LP notes, and follow-up threads; producing searchable records for compliance

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** You've written a lot. Almost none of it is useful when you need it. Notes are scattered across apps, disconnected from each other, and buried under time.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Notion/Obsidian/Apple Notes: passive storage, no AI synthesis, manual linking
- Generic AI (ChatGPT): doesn't know what you've written; answers from the internet, not your brain
- Full-text search: keyword matching, not meaning-matching; still requires you to know what you're looking for
**What it costs them:** Lost ideas, wasted research time, proposals that don't reflect everything they know, decisions made without context they already had
**Emotional tension:** "I know I wrote this down somewhere" -- the frustration of having done the work but being unable to retrieve it

## Competitive Landscape
**Positioning note:** SoNoted is creating a new category -- knowledge activation -- not competing inside an existing one. The competitive frame below is for reference when prospects ask "how is this different from X?" not for head-to-head positioning.

**Legacy note tools (Notion, Obsidian, Apple Notes):** Passive storage. Notes go in; nothing comes out unless you manually retrieve it. No AI synthesis. No cross-app linking.
**AI chat tools (ChatGPT, Claude):** Brilliant, but blind to everything you've written. Answers from the internet, not from your brain.
**Rewind / Mem:** Closer, but narrower. Rewind is screen capture. Mem is single-app. Neither activates your existing multi-tool ecosystem.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- Semantic search (meaning, not keywords)
- Semantic search (meaning, not keywords) -- works on notes you've already written
- Cross-app ingestion (Notion, Obsidian, Apple Notes, plain markdown) -- aspirational roadmap
- Automatic linking -- surfaces connections you didn't make consciously
- Data sovereignty -- your notes never train anyone else's model
**How we do it differently:** Treats your existing note ecosystem as the input, not the thing to replace. SoNoted becomes the intelligence layer on top of everything you've already built.
**Why that's better:** Your past work becomes useful retroactively. You don't change how you write; you change what your notes can do.
**Why customers choose us:** The only product whose entire premise is activating your existing knowledge -- not storing new knowledge, not chatting with the internet.

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I already use Notion / Obsidian" | SoNoted works with them — import your notes, keep your workflow |
| "I don't trust AI with my notes" | Bring your own AI key. Your notes never train our models or anyone else's. |
| "I'll just use ChatGPT" | ChatGPT doesn't know what you wrote. SoNoted does. |

**Anti-persona:** Someone who doesn't take notes, or who takes notes and is happy leaving them siloed. Teams needing collaborative shared wikis (that's Notion's lane, not ours).

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Frustration of not being able to find notes; wasted time re-researching things you already knew; feeling like note-taking is wasted effort
**Pull:** The promise that every note becomes useful -- retroactively. Not just future notes.
**Habit:** Entrenched in existing tool (Notion workflow, Obsidian vault, etc.)
**Anxiety:** "Another app to maintain" / "what happens to my data" / "will this actually work for my notes"
